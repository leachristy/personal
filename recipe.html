Lea's Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients











Instructions

  1. Heat up the pan with vegetable oil
  2. Add the sliced onions and minced garli over high heat for 2 minutes
  3. Cook the pork belly until golden
  4. Set the pork belly aside in a plate and add kimchi in the pan
  5. Add in your rice and gochujang
  6. Mix thoroughly
  7. Add the pork belly in the pan as well as the sugar, green onion, soy sauce, and sesame oil
  8. Mix all together and taste test
  9. Add in any seasoning needed
  10. OPTIONAL: cook a fried egg and add on top
And your done! Enjoy :)
